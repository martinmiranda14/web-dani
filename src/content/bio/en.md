p1: "Danitza Villarroel Orrego is an orchestra conductor, violinist, teacher, and cultural manager. She holds a degree in Art, Technology, and Music Management from the University of Valparaíso, a Diploma in Youth and Children's Orchestra Conducting, and a Diploma in Cultural Heritage with a double specialization from the Alberto Hurtado University. She has received orchestral conducting training from maestros such as Rodolfo Fischer, Helmuth Reichel, Paolo Bortolameolli, and Emmanuel Siffert."
p2: "Coming from public education and a humble family, she discovered her passion between the ages of five and seven at the Ramón Barros Luco School in Valparaíso. Today she leads orchestral projects that democratize classical culture and make visible the role of women in musical conducting."
p3: "She is the founder and conductor of the Alimapu Philharmonic Orchestra (2017 – Present) and the Epic Orchestra (2023 – 2024). She has conducted the Antofagasta Symphony Orchestra, the Valdivia Chamber Orchestra, and the Santiago Municipal Chamber Orchestra. As a violinist, she has recorded with Ensamble Trasatlántico, Luxferre, Charawilla, and Seba Vergara, and is currently a member of the symphonic rock band Pronoias and collaborates with Banda Halekin."
